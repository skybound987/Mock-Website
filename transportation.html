TANITI ISLAND TRANSPORTATION AND LOCAL TRANSIT

Nearly all visitors arrive to Taniti Island by air, though some arrive by sea at the Yellow Leaf Dock. Taniti Airport is small but highly accommodating, and mostly serves smaller jets and propeller planes, however, plans for expansion are currently underway to accommodate larger aircraft. Ground transportation services are the primary resource for most visitors. Taxis, rental cars, and buses can be found near the airport, in addition to being centrally located within the city limits. Biking, hiking, and walking remain very popular options as Taniti is known for its easily walkable terrain and temperate climate. Merriton Landing of Yellow Leaf Bay remains the most sought-after location on the island.

TANITI ISLAND AIRPORT AND TERMINAL

While the Taniti Island Airport and Terminal is the only airport on the island, it serves as the primary gateway and thousands of visitors flow the terminals each year. The airport accommodates small jets and propeller planes, though expansion is planned as tourism grows. Travelers passing through can enjoy fresh island fare at many kiosks or have a drink at the bar.

YELLOW LEAF DOCK AND SEAPORT

Yellow Leaf Dock is the main port of entry by sea, and serves many vessels, including world famous cruise liners. Fishing boats and private charters are no exception for Yellow Leaf Dock, and you'll find a plethora of options for your travel and leisure demands. The primary facilities are currently planned for expansion to accommodate more maritime travelers in the coming years.

PUBLIC AND PRIVATE TRANSIT

Experience the near limitless options that Taniti has to offer, with reliable networks that are efficient and provide maximum comfort. Choose from taxis, rental cars, buses, biking, or even traveling on foot. Public transit is often centrally located and has easy access to the most popular areas of the city, while private transit connects travelers with Taniti's more remote attractions and scenic vistas. Public buses mainly serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily.

TAXIS

Taxis are readily available in nearly all the most popular locations in the city. Our drivers are friendly, professional, and highly in tune with the local culture and most sought-after outlets on the island.

RENTAL CARS AND OTHER VEHICLES

Rental cars are a very popular option for travelers on Taniti Island. This service is popular because it facilitates independence for visitors and allows more freedom. There are many service providers to choose from, ranging from basic to luxury vehicles. The rental car business on Taniti Island is quite large, which results in competitive pricing, allowing visitors more budget friendly options.

BIKING

Cycling is a fantastic way to experience Taniti's beauty, with rental bikes available at multiple vendors throughout the city. Helmets are still required by law on Taniti and are often included for an additional fee. Taniti is known for its flat, bikeable terrain and designated bike paths. Cycling is one of the best ways to experience the tropical climate and atmosphere of Taniti.

EXPLORE ON FOOT

Many of Taniti's most popular attractions are accessible on foot, making it an ideal paradise for pedestrians, hikers, and backpackers. Merriton Landing is a highly walkable area, suited to weary travelers who want to find the best local restaurants, cultural sites, and nightlife. Maps are often offered to travelers for free at many visitor centers and kiosks located around the city.
