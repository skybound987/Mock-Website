WELCOME TO THE ISLAND OF TANITI

Greetings and Welcome to the best place on the web for tourism and travel information for the Island of Taniti!

In the heart of the Pacific Ocean lies the small remote island of Taniti.

The island is approximately 500 square miles of lush tropical rainforest, wrapped with white sand beaches and crystal blue water.

Mountainous areas include an active volcano.

The indigenous population of Taniti ranges about 20,000 people and was dominated by fishing and agriculture in the past.

Today, Taniti Island's economy relies heavily on tourism, and is one of the most sought out islands on the planet.
